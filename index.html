Charity project

We are an organization dedicated to creating blockchain bears that inspire change. A percentage of each bear sale goes towards helping rebuild bears' natural habitats. Currently, their habitats are declining at a great rate. Soon the bears will have nowhere left to live. They will be, forced to seek food and shelter in already overcrowded cities. Wild bears are being exploited for cash performances and forced to live in cages. That causes them to be aggressive. Left in the wild with ample food, bears' can be docile and gentle. By buying a block_bears you are saying yes to change. When we rebuild the bears' natural habitats, we are also rebuilding the ecosystem that surrounds them. That restores balance to our ecosystem, allowing us to coexist in harmony. In the beginning stages, it will be a smaller amount. We plan on building block_bears further. So that we can bring in more profit for the charity, in the long run, we will vote on the exact amount and discuss any questions, ideas, or concerns. We will be holding a community vote for the charity we will be donating. Thank you for all your contributions and for sharing our project with your loved ones.
