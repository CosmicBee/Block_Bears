About
Block_Bears is a 100% unique individually hand-crafted utility voxel bears, dedicated to inspiring change. There are 10,000 lovable bears in search of their forever home. Our community is a place where you can express your fandom and have your voice be truly heard and valued. A place where we can be environmentally conscious and fun roams-free. Our goal is to make a genuinely positive impact on our environment while providing bears their habitats back. With each purchase, a percentage will be donated to pull bears out of a terrible situation and rebuild their natural homes. The rest will go towards funding the block_bears metaverse game.

Charity project

We are an organization dedicated to creating blockchain bears that inspire change. A percentage of each bear sale goes towards helping build bear sanctuarys. Currently, their habitats are declining at a great rate. Soon the bears will have nowhere left to live. They will be, forced to seek food and shelter in already overcrowded cities. Wild bears are being exploited for cash performances and forced to live in cages. That causes them to be aggressive. Left in the wild with ample food, bears' can be docile and gentle. By buying a block_bears you are saying yes to change. When we rebuild the bears' natural habitats, we are also rebuilding the ecosystem that surrounds them. That restores balance to our ecosystem, allowing us to coexist in harmony. Thank you for all your contributions and for sharing our project with your loved ones.
